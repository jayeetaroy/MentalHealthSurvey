About

Currently over 1400 responses, the ongoing 2016 survey aims to measure attitudes towards mental health in the tech workplace, and examine the frequency of mental health disorders among tech workers.

The survey will be used in gauging how mental health is viewed within the tech/IT workplace, and the prevalence of certain mental health disorders within the tech industry.

The web application created through this project will helps us visualize the findings of the survey.
